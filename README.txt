    Description:                                                                   ║
A modded pump-action shotgun.                                                      ║
Developed in the late XIX century, Winchester model 97 quickly became popular      ║
among hunters, competitive shooters and eventually soldiers,                       ║
who relied on it's reliability and firepower to survive the trench warfare of WW1  ║
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╦══╝
    Installation:                                                               ║
Copy WinchesterPatch folder to BeInEx/Plugins                                   ║
Copy Winchester_1897 folder to %AppData%/LocalLow/Wolfire Games/Receiver2/Guns  ║
 Create the Guns folder if necessary                                            ║
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╦═╝
    Features:                                                                 ║
 - Pump action operation                                                      ║
 - Special 12 gauge shell                                                     ║
 - Every shot fires 8 pellets with maximum deviation of 2 deg to either side  ║
